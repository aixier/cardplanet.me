Copyright & Intellectual Property Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Important Notice

This policy outlines the intellectual property rights and copyright guidelines for CardPlanet users. By using our service, you agree to respect these terms and the intellectual property rights of others.

1. Content Ownership

1.1 Your Content

When you create cards using CardPlanet, you retain full ownership of:

  • The original text and content you input into our system
  • Any personal images or materials you upload (when this feature is available)
  • The specific creative choices and combinations you make

1.2 Generated Cards

Cards created using CardPlanet are considered your intellectual property, subject to the following conditions:

  • You have the right to use, share, and commercialize cards you create
  • You are responsible for ensuring your content doesn't infringe on others' rights
  • CardPlanet retains a non-exclusive license to use generated cards for service improvement

1.3 CardPlanet Property

The following remain the exclusive property of CardPlanet:

  • The CardPlanet platform, software, and underlying AI technology
  • Design templates and style algorithms
  • The CardPlanet brand, logo, and marketing materials
  • System-generated design elements and patterns

2. Usage Rights & Licenses

2.1 Commercial Use

You are granted the right to use cards created with CardPlanet for:

  • Personal and commercial projects
  • Social media content and marketing materials
  • Educational and informational purposes
  • Client work and business presentations

2.2 Restrictions

You may NOT:

  • Claim exclusive ownership of CardPlanet's design styles or templates
  • Reverse engineer or copy CardPlanet's AI algorithms
  • Use CardPlanet to create content that infringes on others' copyrights
  • Resell or redistribute CardPlanet's templates as standalone products
  • Remove or obscure any CardPlanet watermarks (if applicable to your plan)

3. Third-Party Content

3.1 User Responsibility

When using CardPlanet, you are responsible for:

  • Ensuring you have the right to use any text, images, or content you input
  • Not violating any copyrights, trademarks, or intellectual property rights
  • Obtaining necessary permissions for any third-party content
  • Complying with all applicable copyright laws in your jurisdiction

3.2 Copyright Infringement

CardPlanet respects intellectual property rights and expects users to do the same. If you believe your copyright has been infringed:

  • Contact us immediately with details of the infringement
  • Provide proof of ownership and specific examples
  • We will investigate and take appropriate action

4. DMCA Compliance

4.1 Notice and Takedown Procedure

CardPlanet complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). To file a DMCA notice, please provide:

  1. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
  2. Identification of the material claimed to be infringing
  3. Your contact information (name, address, email, phone)
  4. A statement of good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  5. A statement of accuracy and authorization to act on behalf of the owner
  6. Your physical or electronic signature

4.2 Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was wrongly removed, you may file a counter-notice including:

  1. Identification of the material that was removed
  2. A statement of good faith belief that the removal was in error
  3. Your name, address, and consent to jurisdiction
  4. Your physical or electronic signature

5. Fair Use Policy

CardPlanet supports fair use of copyrighted materials for:

  • Educational and teaching purposes
  • Commentary and criticism
  • Parody and transformative works
  • News reporting and journalism

However, fair use determinations are complex and fact-specific. Users are responsible for understanding and applying fair use principles appropriately.

6. Attribution Requirements

6.1 When Using Third-Party Content

If your cards include third-party content, ensure proper attribution:

  • Credit original authors and sources clearly
  • Include copyright notices where required
  • Follow Creative Commons or other license requirements
  • Respect attribution preferences of content creators

6.2 CardPlanet Attribution

Depending on your subscription plan:

  • Free users: Cards may include a small CardPlanet watermark
  • Premium users: No watermark required, but attribution is appreciated
  • Enterprise users: Custom branding options available

7. International Copyright

CardPlanet operates globally and respects international copyright laws:

  • We comply with the Berne Convention for the Protection of Literary and Artistic Works
  • We respect copyright laws in all countries where we operate
  • Users are responsible for complying with their local copyright laws
  • Cross-border copyright issues will be handled according to applicable treaties
