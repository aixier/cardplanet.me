Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CardPlanet

What is CardPlanet?
CardPlanet is an AI-powered creative card design platform that helps content creators make stunning knowledge cards in just 3 minutes. With 12 professional design styles, it's perfect for Xiaohongshu, Instagram, and Pinterest.
How long does it take to create a card?
With CardPlanet's AI technology, you can create a professional-looking card in just 3 minutes. Simply choose your style, input your content, and let AI do the rest.
Do I need design skills to use CardPlanet?
No design skills are needed! CardPlanet is designed for everyone. Our AI handles all the design work - you just need to provide the content.
What design styles are available?
CardPlanet offers 12 professional design styles including System Clean, Impressionist Soft, Geometric Symphony, Minimal, Pin Board, Editorial Soft, Luxe Print, Woven Texture, Cosmic Empire, Fluid Zen, Chinese Artistry, and Bubble Fresh.
Is CardPlanet free to use?
Yes, CardPlanet offers a free plan to get started. You can create cards with basic features at no cost. Premium features and unlimited creations are available with paid plans.
What social media platforms does CardPlanet support?
CardPlanet creates cards optimized for all major social media platforms including Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book), Instagram, Pinterest, Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn. All cards use the optimal 3:4 aspect ratio.
Can I customize the templates?
Yes! While our AI generates the initial design, you can customize colors, fonts, layouts, and add your own branding elements to make each card uniquely yours.
How does the AI work?
CardPlanet's AI analyzes your content and automatically selects the best layout, color scheme, and typography based on your chosen style. It ensures optimal readability and visual appeal for maximum engagement.
Can I download cards in high resolution?
Yes, all cards can be downloaded in high resolution suitable for any platform. Premium users get access to ultra-high resolution exports and multiple format options.
Is there a limit to how many cards I can create?
Free users can create up to 20 cards per month. Premium plans offer increased limits or unlimited card creation depending on your subscription level.
